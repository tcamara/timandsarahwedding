The Next Chapter


A Wedding

by

Tim Camara  &  Sarah Baugher

Once upon a time, a boy and a girl met on the first day of high school.
For the girl, it was love at first sight. The boy didn't even seem to notice. It wasn't exactly your typical storybook romance.
After graduation, the girl moved away. Increased distance showed them what they had been taking for granted. Although now separated by 1200 miles, their relationship blossomed.
Three years and a stack of cell phone bills later, they were finally reunited for good. They moved in together, bought some Ikea furniture, and started going on adventures. They even adopted a lion-hearted kitty.
Now they're inviting you to help them write the next chapter.
